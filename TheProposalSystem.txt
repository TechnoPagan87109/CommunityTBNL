The Community operates under a set of by-laws

Adding, removing or altering by-laws begins with a proposal

Any community member may make a proposal

Once a proposal is made any member may make comments, edits or ratings, or they can decide that they have no interest in the proposal

Those who decide they have no interest in the proposal aren't counted. Reversing this decision can be done at any time

Software converts proposal to word vectored spaces

Ratings are normalized

Multi-Objective Particle Swarm Optimization (MOPSO) is used to predict language for the Proposal that will score highest level of satasfactin by all interested members while identifying irreconscible areas

The new form of the proposal is made availible for members to make new comments, edits and ratings

This is repeted until there are no objections and a minimum satisfaction level is reached. Higher satisfaction levels may be required depending on the Proposal. Once reached the Proposal becomes a new by-law

Gamification is use to close the gap between dissatisfaction and action
