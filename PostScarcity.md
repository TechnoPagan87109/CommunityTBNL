For the purpose of this project, a Post-Scarcity society is defined as:
a society where [Autonomous Labor](https://github.com/TechnoPagan87109/CommunityTBNL/tree/Blueprint/AutonomousLabor.txt)
collects the [energy](https://github.com/TechnoPagan87109/CommunityTBNL/tree/Blueprint/Energy.txt) and
[raw materials](https://github.com/TechnoPagan87109/CommunityTBNL/tree/Blueprint/RawMaterials.txt) necessary to
produce the [goods and services](https://github.com/TechnoPagan87109/CommunityTBNL/tree/Blueprint/GoodsAndServices.txt)
that society needs at [virtually no cost](https://github.com/TechnoPagan87109/CommunityTBNL/tree/Blueprint/VirtuallyNoCost.txt)
