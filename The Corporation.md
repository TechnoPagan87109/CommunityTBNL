The legal structure for the Community is a [Member Benifits non-profit corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mutual-benefit_nonprofit_corporation)

There are no shareholders, only Members. Profits are only distributed to the Community members as [assignable resources](AssignableResources.txt)

Members are owners and employees of the Corporation as well as members of the Community. You can't be one without the other two

The Corporation ownes the resorces, while the Members jointly own the Corporation

By-Laws, in the form of Smart Contracts determin how resources how resources are used and shared

Members are able to assign their share of resources as they choose, to create consumer goods, income streams, housing and personal production resources

Each Member has equal say in the decisions of the Community and is equally responsible for running the Community
