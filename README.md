Welcome to the CommunityTBNL (ToBeNamedLater) repository!

This project aims to facilitate collaboration on the development of an open-source, replicable, near Post-Scarcity ecovillage. Our goal is to create a sustainable community where autonomous labor, combined with the utilization of energy and raw materials, can produce all the goods and services needed by this community.

What is Post-Scarcity?

In this context, Post-Scarcity refers to a society in which autonomous labor efficiently gathers the necessary resources and energy to produce the goods and services that society needs at virtually no cost

An Emulation of Post-Scarcity 

At present, it is not feasible for a small, self-funded community to afford the infrastructure required for full Post-Scarcity. Therefore, our approach focuses on an emulation of Post-Scarcity that we can call, near Post-Scarcity.

Collaborative Learning and Labor

Since automation and AI technologies have not yet reached a point where all processes can be automated, community members will contribute their labor in various areas. The diverse skill set required will encompass disciplines such as metalwork, weaving, gardening, construction, and more. We would encourage community members to develop a variety of those skill sets to do things they enjoy and aim to continually automate processes to handle more and more of the work they'd rather not do.

Dependencies and Outsourcing

While our community strives for self-sufficiency, there are certain items that cannot be produced within a small community at this stage. The most notable examples are Large Scale Integrated Circuit devices, including processors, SSD memory, GPUs, and alike. To address this, we will rely on the proceeds from surplus items we can produce within the community. Sales of surplus production on the open market will provide the necessary funds to acquire these externally produced items. It is also worth noting that there will always be certain expenses, such as tax payments, subscriptions, and licenses, which still require cash.
