Energy, harvested from the environment by autonomous labor supplies first the community, then with a surplus that can be fed into the grid for working capital. The Corporation should work to become an electric cooperative to deal with the grid and other power companies.

Different properties will have different harvestable energy and the community blueprint should reflect this.

Some available renewable energy include solar. While solar panels may be impossible for the Community to produce initially, alternatives exist such as [Solar Thermal Energy](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_thermal_energy)

Wind is another source of renewable energy. One of the machines Open Source Ecology has in the planning stages is a [50kW Wind Turbine](https://www.opensourceecology.org/portfolio/50-kw-wind-turbine/). There are plans available for various types of vertical and horizontal turbines which will have advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific property.

If the property has water running through it [MicroHydro Power](https://www.energy.gov/energysaver/microhydropower-systems) becomes an option.
